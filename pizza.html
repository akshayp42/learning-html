Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

  • 200g pkt Coles Thin & Crispy Medium Pizza Bases (2 pack)
  • 1/3 cup (95g) pizza tomato paste
  • 1 cup (120g) Coles Australian Shredded Pizza Blend Cheese
  • 75g Coles Pepperoni Salami, casing removed, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbs Coles Italia Basilico Basil Pesto

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C. Place the pizza bases on 2 greased baking trays. Spread with the pizza paste.

  2. Top the pizza bases with the cheese and pepperoni.

  3. Bake for 12 mins or until the pizza bases are golden brown and the cheese melts. Drizzle with pesto to serve.
